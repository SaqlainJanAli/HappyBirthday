Today is...

as beautiful as any other day,

but today you realize

another year has flown by

in the blink of an eye.

However...

Do you know..?

today is not just special...

it’s incredibly, extraordinarily special to you,

and that's why

you should make it...

the best celebration ever.

Let me share...

a piece of my heart, a slice of my joy with you.

Thank you for always being there,

Whenever I need any help.

Thank you for every help you gave me and the love.

thank you for everything.

I wish you all the best

May your life be filled with ease,

and may all your wishes come true.

Remember...

your dreams,

and live as a free bird...

soaring high in the vast blue sky.

I hope...

you find happiness along the way,

keep your spirit high,

and savor every single moment...

you experience today.

Fill it with your most beautiful smile,

and create the best memories.

Lastly...

I'd like to wish you,

one more time,

a very happy birthday.
